About Me

Hi! I'm Johnny, a software engineer. I'm 19 years old and from Chicago, Illinois. I attend the Univerity of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and majoring in Computer Engineering. I first started coding freshman year of high school and have created many projects since. Through my four computer science classes in high school, I was introduced to a wide variety of computer science fields such as websites, mobile apps, machine learning, artificial intelligence, data processing, and even games. I particularly enjoy building apps that have a useful purpose and impact my community. I spent a year making an app to help new students navigate the halls of my high school using a 3D outline of my school. The app was released on the students' school-provided iPads and is still in use today. Through my experiences, I learned how to develop projects from start to finish. I'm continuing to develop more projects and look forward to introducing to the world a new form of innovation.
